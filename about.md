  <h2>Our Mission</h2>
  <p>To bring the light of God's Word to every heart — illuminating minds, transforming lives, and building a community rooted in Scripture.</p>

  <h2>What We Do</h2>
  <ul>
    <li><strong>Bible Studies</strong> — In-depth study of Scripture, book by book, verse by verse.</li>
    <li><strong>Discipleship</strong> — Walking alongside believers as they grow in their faith.</li>
    <li><strong>Community Outreach</strong> — Sharing God's love through acts of service and generosity.</li>
    <li><strong>Online Resources</strong> — Blog posts, devotionals, and study materials available to everyone.</li>
  </ul>

  <h2>Our Beliefs</h2>
  <p>We believe the Bible is the inspired, inerrant Word of God — our final authority in all matters of faith and practice. We believe in the triune God: Father, Son, and Holy Spirit. We believe in salvation through faith in Jesus Christ alone, and that His Great Commission is our calling.</p>

  <h2>Join Us</h2>
  <p>Whether you're a lifelong believer or just beginning to explore faith, you're welcome here. Let's study the Word together.</p>
